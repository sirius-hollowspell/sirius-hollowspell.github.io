New Piercing

Sirius Hollowspell
2021-12-11
piercing

Today was a very exciting day! The first thing we did today was go to my swim meet, although I didn’t drop time in any of my events, I still thought it was a good time. After I had finished my events at the swim meet, I went up to the spectator area to sit with Daddy while the 500 freestyles were being finished and decided that today I would be getting my ears pierced. We went to the mall and ate at the food court after the meet. We also had an escape room planned in the same mall, so while we were waiting for it we looked around the mall for a place that did piercings, and we found one! So we scheduled a piercing and a few minutes later, I got the piercing!

Our escape room had the theme of “Island Escape”, it was challenging but also really fun! We were able to make it out of the room with 3 minutes left, so it was a close one!
