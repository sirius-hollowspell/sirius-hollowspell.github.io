Thanksgiving Cruise Day 2

Sirius Hollowspell
2021-11-21
windjammer

After waking up, we watched us dock in the Nassau port, we headed to the Windjammer for breakfast and enjoyed some buffet bacon. The bacon in the Windjammer is AMAZING! If you ever go on a Royal Caribbean cruise, definitely check out the Windjammer. After breakfast, we went to our reservation for the North Star, and it was pretty awesome to see Nassau from up there. We then went and chilled by the pool in the infinity spa. It was cool seeing the pool deck from a higher floor. Once we were done and dried off, we headed to the room so I could prepare for iFly. After we got dressed, I was ready, so we went to the back of the ship to do some indoor skydiving at the iFly. After I flew in the wind tunnel, which was thrilling, we went over to the Windjammer for lunch. We didn’t have any plans until Giovanni’s Italian Kitchen at dinner, so we just chilled in the room until then. Eventually, while we were in the room, we sailed away from Nassau and I took some beautiful pictures.
