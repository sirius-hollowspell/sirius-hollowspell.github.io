Thanksgiving Cruise Day 4

Sirius Hollowspell
2021-11-23
casita, windjammer

As per usual, we went to the Windjammer for breakfast and then went to the pool deck to check out the casita we purchased for this day. After checking in for the casita, they told us it would be ready at 10:00, so we went to our room to relax until then. Once it was 10:00 and our casita was ready, Mama and I went to it while Daddy ran laps around the track on board. We did see him pass by our casita a couple of times. Once Daddy was done running, we all went back to our room to dry off and dress for lunch.
