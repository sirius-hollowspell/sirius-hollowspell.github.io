Thanksgiving Cruise Day 1

Sirius Hollowspell
2021-11-20
windjammer

When we woke up in our hotel in Fort Lauderdale, we were ready as ever to board our cruise, the Odyssey of the Seas! There were also quite a lot of cruise ships waiting to board. We were boarding the Odyssey of the Seas at 11:00, so when it was 10:30, we took a Lyft over to the terminal and got ready to board. We were hungry when we boarded, so we went and found the Windjammer and had lunch there. After lunch, we checked out our suite, and it was pretty nice. Then we reserved spots in the fun activities aboard the Odyssey of the Seas. We walked around the ship trying to explore everything, and we found the spa! We got a tour around it and saw the different rooms they have for different types of treatment, and it smelled very nice. At the other end of the ship, there was Playmakers, which is a sports bar and arcade. Daddy got some drinks and watched a football game while Mama and I played some rounds of pool. I beat Mama in the first game, but she won in the second. After walking around the Playmakers and playing some arcade games like skeeball, we found out they were doing bumper cars at 3:00 pm. After doing bumper cars, we watched the sailaway form Fort Lauderdale. After leaving the harbor, the ship became incredibley rocky.
