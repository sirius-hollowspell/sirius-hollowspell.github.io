Thanksgiving Cruise Day 3

Sirius Hollowspell
2021-11-22
suite, suite lounge, windjammer

We didn’t have many plans for a while after we woke up, so we decided to get some breakfast at the Windjammer, and then after we had finished eating, we headed to the pool to relax and get some sun. After a few hours outside, we were hungry, so we went to the Windjammer for lunch again. We went to the suite lounge, and because it’s in the back of the ship, you have a view of where you’ve been. If you get a suite on the Odyssey of the Seas, I recommend it. After chilling in the suite lounge for a few minutes, we had our family photo session. We were able to get some nice-looking pictures! When we had finished taking pictures, we went and ate in the main dining hall.
