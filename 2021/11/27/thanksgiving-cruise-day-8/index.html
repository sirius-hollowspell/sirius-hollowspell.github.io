Thanksgiving Cruise Day 8

Sirius Hollowspell
2021-11-27
q&a, windjammer

Today, it took me a long time to wake up, but eventually, I did wake up. Before we went to the Windjammer, we sat in the room and hung out for a bit. While we were eating, there was an announcement that there would be a Q&A with the captain! So, Mama and I decided to go there after breakfast. After breakfast, Mama and I went to the Q&A and waited for it to start. In the Q&A, the first thing shown was a video of all the areas where passengers usually can’t access, such as the crew sun deck, crew buffet, crew gaming area, and other crew areas. We also had plans to go to the towel animal folding demonstration, so we had to leave the Q&A to get to that on time. For the demonstration, they brought in 5 housekeepers to show how to make five different towel animals. Once the housekeepers finished making the towel animals, they put them on a table so people could take pictures of them, and I got a picture of the towel animals. Afterward, we relaxed at the pool and got some more sun on our last day aboard. We went to our usual lunch after being at the pool, the Windjammer. Mama and I went to the room after lunch to get ready for the last bingo game of the cruise while Daddy watched football in the room. Unfortunately, we didn’t win any money in this game, although we still enjoyed it. We went to the Focus Photo Gallery after bingo to select and download ten prepaid photos. After downloading our pictures, we went back to the room and got ready for our last dinner aboard, Giovanni’s. It was very delicious, and as if I haven’t said it enough, you MUST try Giovanni’s if you ever cruise with Royal Caribbean. After dinner, we went back to our room to get ready for our final sleep on the Odyssey of the Seas.
